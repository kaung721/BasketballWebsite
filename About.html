About Baytown Basketball Club

Building champions on and off the court since 2009

Our Story

Founded in 2009, Baytown Basketball Club began as a small community initiative with a simple goal: to provide local youth with access to quality basketball training and competition.

What started as a group of 12 dedicated players has grown into one of the most respected basketball programs in the region, with over 500 athletes having passed through our doors. Our success is measured not just in wins and championships, but in the character development and life skills we instill in every player.
